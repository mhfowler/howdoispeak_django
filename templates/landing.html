How Do You Speak?

For my Data Science final project, I am building an app which analyzes the words you use in your text messages and tells you things about yourself. The app will answer questions like:

  • which words do you use most frequently compared to average?
  • which friends do you send the happiest sounding texts to?
  • how often do you use words that have to do with politics/sports/partying/studying compared to your friends?

The more data I have to work with the better the data analysis will be. Anyone who uses an iPhone and backs there iPhone up on a Mac can participate (and I will send you your results!). To participate in the project all you have to do is:

  1. Click the download link below (it will download a .zip file).
  2. Double click howdoispeak.app.zip to unzip it.
  3. Right click on howdoispeak.app and select open to securely upload your SMS word frequency data to the HowDoISpeak server.
